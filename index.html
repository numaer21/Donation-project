Our Donation Plan

When you donate to the poor, you are giving the gift of hope to countless men, women and children who comes through our door every day looking for a helping hand and a path to a brighter future.

Food for a family

Sometimes, the family members of a serious patient or of a flood effected do not get the opportunity to have food or to look after them. For which we are raising a fund in order to help these kind of family with our support in order to get them back on track.

Shelter for a family

Not everyone who is homeless is a drug-addict or in need of any mental medical attention. Some are normal people like us who have been knocked down financially or by family, not everyone in the street makes a bad choice in life, some wants to come back around and for them we will be trying our best to support them in every way possible.

Education for a child

Education is the foremost important aspect for everyone. But for a child education is necessary as they absobs the basics of everything quickly. The poor people dosen't seem to understand the concept and forces their children to work and earn the bread of the family at an early age for which they remain uneducated and cannot grow in life. And that is why we are trying to get people know about education and the power of it and help the children to go to school for which we are raising the fund necessary for it.
